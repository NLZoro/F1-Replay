This data is then used to create a realistic and immersive racing experience, complete with realistic car handling, track conditions, and weather effects. The game also features a range of customizable options, including playback speed, camera angles, and graphics settings, allowing players to tailor their experience to their preferences.

The game includes features such as leaderboards, multiplayer support, and social media sharing, making it easy for players to compete with friends, share their progress, and join a community of like-minded racing enthusiasts.
